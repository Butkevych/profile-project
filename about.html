Hello, I am Daria Butkevych

Front-end developer-beginner based in Ukraine

My name is Daria Butkevych. I am a professional volleyball player. I have been playing volleyball for 13 years. Also I graduated from university with a Bachelor and Master's degrees. My specialties were translation and pedagogy. Although I know a lot of things for this moment, I always look for something new. New hobbies, knowledge, adventures, trips. Once when I was in France and I had no opportunity to go home, I started to work a lot and to discover myself again. That moment was fatal. I found a new passion: coding. Coding is something special for me. It helps me to connect my love to create art and the desire to organize everything, to be romantic, naïve and at the same time to use my analytical brain, to combine my writing and photography, showing this world my little secrets. This site will reflect all my plans, all my works and actually a great part of me. So welcome and have a great journey in Daria Butkevych’s universe.
